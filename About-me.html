M9k Remastered

A complete overhaul of a very popular Garry's Mod Addon. M9k Remastered (open source) features all of the original M9k weapon packs but the old code was scrapped and replaced by new modern code that is far more optimized and more friendly for low-end Computers and Servers. Additionally it was also re-balanced for the DarkRP Gamemode to be more fair and engaging to turn gun-fights into actual fights that are fun instead of just a one-shot drop-dead situation.

M9k Remastered has 23,508 Subscribers and 684 ratings of which 604 are positive (88%)*. It is very popular among DarkRP and realism role-playing servers.

* Statistics are from the day of writing this (2nd December 2021), these will eventually be updated again in the future.
